In this episode of the Üretim Bandı podcast, Fatih Bildirici from HAVELSAN delves into the intricacies of developing software that adheres to stringent standards. He discusses the roles and responsibilities of a software configuration manager, the importance of code reviews and Pull Requests (PR), and the evolving nature of industry standards. Fatih also touches on the significance of rituals and onboarding processes in maintaining quality and consistency. Additionally, he explores the challenges and benefits of long-term projects, the technologies employed, and the differences between working in a start-up versus a corporate environment.
