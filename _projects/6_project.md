# Decoding Intelligence: Foundations and Applications of Machine Learning and Deep Learning

### 🧠 **Presentation Overview**
This presentation explores the historical evolution, foundational concepts, and modern applications of machine learning and deep learning, highlighting how these technologies have shaped the present and will impact the future.

---

### 📌 **Topics**
1. **Historical Evolution**  
   - From the origins of artificial intelligence to today’s advancements.  
   - The role of pattern recognition and statistical foundations.  

2. **Machine Learning**  
   - Supervised, unsupervised, and reinforcement learning paradigms.  
   - Black-box models and Explainable AI (XAI).  

3. **Deep Learning**  
   - Modern architectures like CNN, RNN, and Transformers.  
   - Applications in healthcare, language processing, and autonomous systems.
