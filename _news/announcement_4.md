Honored to have presented at **AICon0.2** hosted by **Ankara University’s YazGit (Artificial Intelligence and Image Processing Club)**! 🚀 In my session, "Demystifying Machine Intelligence: The Path to Explainable AI," I shared insights into **Explainable AI (XAI)** techniques that bring transparency and trust to complex AI models. We covered AI’s evolution from simple rule-based systems to today’s powerful black-box models, emphasizing XAI’s essential role in industries that rely on accountable and interpretable AI. 📘✨ Excited to contribute to the conversation on ethical and transparent AI!
